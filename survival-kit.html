War Survival Kit

Essential gear and supplies for emergency preparedness

By Hazem ElBatawy

A comprehensive survival kit is crucial during conflict scenarios. This guide covers essential gear, supplies, and tools organized by priority and function.

Emergency Essentials (First 72 Hours)

Essential medical supplies and emergency gear for the first 72 hours

  • Water & Hydration
    • 3L water per person/day (minimum)
    • Water filters or purification tablets
    • Collapsible water containers
  • Food & Nutrition
    • High-calorie emergency rations
    • Dried foods and protein bars
    • Manual can opener
  • First Aid & Medical
    • Comprehensive first aid kit
    • Prescription medications
    • Wound care supplies
    • Emergency blankets

Navigation & Communication

  • Navigation Tools
    • Paper maps (local area)
    • Compass (non-electronic)
    • Signal mirror
  • Communication
    • Hand-crank radio
    • Whistle or sound signals
    • Emergency contact information

Protection & Shelter

Essential weatherproofing and shelter equipment for protection against the elements

  • Protective Gear
  • N95 masks or respirators
  • Work gloves
  • Eye protection
  • Shelter Items
    • Emergency shelter or tarp
    • Rope or paracord
    • Emergency blankets
    • Basic tools (multi-tool)

    • Documents & Resources

    • Critical Documents
      • ID and important papers (waterproof)
      • Emergency contacts list
      • Area maps and safe routes
    • Financial
      • Emergency cash (small bills)
      • Copies of important documents

    Additional Considerations

    • Power & Light
      • Hand-crank flashlight
      • Extra batteries
      • Solar charger (if essential)
    • Hygiene & Sanitation
      • Personal hygiene items
      • Sanitation supplies
      • Portable toilet supplies

    Field Navigation Quick Guide

    Essentials for navigating without GPS: compass + paper map + natural north.

    • Compass: Take a bearing, correct for magnetic declination (e.g., Cairo ~ +3¬∞E in 2025), and follow distant aiming points.
    • Map: Orient the map to north; triangulate with two back-bearings from visible landmarks.
    • Natural North: Shadow-stick (day), Polaris (night). Sun is due south at local solar noon in the Northern Hemisphere.

    Kit Maintenance

    • Check expiration dates monthly
    • Rotate food and water supplies
    • Test all equipment quarterly
    • Update documents as needed
    • Adjust kit based on seasonal needs

    Insects & Snakes Protection

    • Insect Protection
      • Insect repellent (DEET or picaridin-based)
      • Mosquito netting
      • Permethrin-treated clothing
      • Sting relief pads or cream
    • Snake Safety
      • Snake bite kit (suction device, bandages)
      • Heavy-duty gloves for handling debris
      • Sturdy boots (for protection when moving through brush)
      • Flashlight (for night movement)
    • First Aid for Bites/Stings
      • Antihistamines for allergic reactions
      • Hydrocortisone cream
      • Compression bandages (for snake bites)
      • Emergency contact numbers for poison control
